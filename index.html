Evening power hour

A 2-3 hour Focus! Ideal for getting in the mood to tackle the bigger projects.

De-clutter Day

Up to 8 whole hours. Making plans to redecorate? sell your home? setting up and staging your buy-to-let property? organise any room you have in mind? Best practices put in place from the beginning

Weekend Warrior

Any two consecutive days of 6 hours each. Tackle the bigger projects. The garage; wouldn't it be lovely to park the car in it and leave the drive car-clutter-free? The attic; don't let it drop into the spare room! Do you really know what all's up there? This package is available on any two consecutive days.

Office Option

Drowning in paper? From experience, we know paperwork takes the most time, hotly followed by boxes of photographs and other memorabilia. 3 - 4 hours will definitely make an impact, and clients have been very pleased with the progress in that time. Two sessions are probably best: The first to sort through the paperwork, the second to file, organise and build-in future-proof methods of dealing with incoming material.
